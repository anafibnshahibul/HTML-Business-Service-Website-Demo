Security Policy (Vulnerability Disclosure)

We take the security of Lumina Premium very seriously. If you have found a vulnerability, please report it responsibly.

Our Rules:
  • Do not perform DDoS attacks.
  • Do not try to steal other users' data.
  • Give us at least 30 days to fix the issue before going public.

How to report?

Please email us at: lumina2026@email.com

Include steps to reproduce the bug and any screenshots if possible.

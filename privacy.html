Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

At Lumina, accessible from your website, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by Lumina and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

When you use our contact form, we collect personal information that you provide to us, such as:

  • Name: To address you personally.
  • Email Address: To respond to your inquiries.
  • Message Content: Any information you choose to provide about your project or query.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect in various ways, including to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our website.
  • Improve, personalize, and expand our website.
  • Understand and analyze how you use our website.
  • Communicate with you, either directly or through one of our partners, including for customer service and updates.

3. Data Storage (Google Forms)

Please note that our contact form is powered by Google Forms. By submitting the form, you acknowledge that the information you provide will be transferred to Google for processing in accordance with their Privacy Policy and Terms.

4. Security

We value your trust in providing us your Personal Information, thus we are striving to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it. But remember that no method of transmission over the internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure and reliable.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at support@lumina.com.
